Chocolate Buttercream Frosting



Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe:

This Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe is the "icing on the cake" for our Decadent Chocolate Cake. Also passed down from my Great-Great Grandmother, this recipe will have you licking the bowl!


Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 cups butter softened
  • 1 cup unsweetened coca powder
  • 5 cups confectioner's sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder

Instructions:

  1. Add cocoa powder to a large bowl or bowl of stand mixer. Whisk through to remove any lumps.

  2. Cream together butter and cocoa powder until well-combined.

  3. Add sugar and milk to cocoa mixture by adding 1 cup of sugar followed by about a tablespoon of milk. After each addition has been combined, turn mixer onto a high speed for about a minute. Repeat until all sugar and milk have been added.

  4. Add vanilla extract and espresso powder and combine well.

  5. If frosting appears too dry, add more milk, a tablespoon at a time until it reaches the right consistency. If it appears to wet and does not hold its form, add more confectionerâ€™s sugar, a tablespoon at a time until it reaches the right consistency.
